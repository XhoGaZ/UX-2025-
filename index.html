Show More

Process

I was tasked with designing a metaverse avatar application for a school. This application was to give students the option to create their own avatar. An avatar they could identify with. With this avatar they would be able to customize their clothes, shape and equipment. Much work went to understanding the needs and pain points of the users. This was achieved through a empathy map and a user flow chart.

My low-fidelity prototype was quickly updated to a medium-fidelity prototype. The next step was getting feedback and refining my work. After careful reflection small changes was made here and there. Many alterations led to new insights that would ultimately transform the design.
